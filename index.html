Hi! I am a researcher worker on Games and automata, in particular those that involve parity conditions or counters. My research so far has been on history-deterministic automata and parity tree automata.

I am a postdoctoral researcher since June 2025 in the MoVe team of LIS, Aix-Marseille Université, under the supervision of Karoliina Lehtinen and Pierre-Alain Reynier. Since June 2026, I am an MSCA postdoctoral fellow.

Before my current postdoctoral position, I was a PhD student at the University of Warwick, with Marcin as my supervisor.
